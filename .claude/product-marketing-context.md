# HireDeveloper.ae - Product Marketing Context

## What We Do
HireDeveloper.ae is a premium talent marketplace that connects UAE/GCC companies with the top 2% of vetted remote developers, designers, marketers, project managers, and assistants. Think "Toptal for the Middle East."

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** CTOs, VP Engineering, hiring managers at UAE-based companies (startups to enterprise)
- **Secondary:** HR teams at companies in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, and broader GCC
- **Industries:** Fintech (DIFC), E-commerce, SaaS, Government/Smart City, Oil & Gas digital transformation, Real estate tech

## Key Value Propositions
1. **Pre-vetted talent** - Only top 2% pass our screening (technical + soft skills)
2. **UAE market expertise** - We understand local business culture, timezones, regulations
3. **Cost savings** - Save 40-60% vs local hiring while maintaining quality
4. **Speed** - Get matched with qualified candidates in 48 hours
5. **Flexibility** - Hire freelance, full-time, or dedicated teams
6. **Real human support** - Dedicated account manager, not just a platform

## Competitive Positioning
- vs Toptal: More affordable, UAE-focused, better local support
- vs Upwork: Higher quality (vetted), more reliable, account management included
- vs Local agencies: More cost-effective, larger talent pool, faster matching

## Conversion Goals
- Primary: Submit lead form (name, email, company, role needed)
- Secondary: Book a consultation call
- Tertiary: Browse talent profiles

## Brand Voice
- Professional but approachable
- Confident without being arrogant
- Data-driven (use specific numbers)
- Local expertise ("we know the UAE market")
- Premium positioning (not the cheapest, but the best value)

## Key Stats (use in content)
- 90,000+ vetted developers in network
- 48-hour candidate matching

- Companies in 6 GCC countries served
- 200+ technologies covered
